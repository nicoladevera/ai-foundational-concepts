**Read after:** Customizing AI

**What you'll learn:** Why command-line AI agents are dramatically more efficient than chat-based AI for complex work

**Sets up:** Understanding of different AI interfaces and their performance characteristics

**Key connection:** After learning to customize AI (page 5), this shows you the most efficient way to actually work with it. You'll understand why architecture matters as much as the AI itself - CLI agents deliver 5-10x speedups not through better models, but through better implementation.

**Read after:** CLI Agents vs. Chat-Based AI Performance

**What you'll learn:** Different architectural patterns for AI applications - from simple automation to autonomous agents

**Sets up:** Strategic framework for choosing how to implement AI in your work

**Key connection:** You know how to use AI efficiently (page 6) - now learn what to build. Should you create simple automation, an AI workflow with human checkpoints, or a fully autonomous agent? This page provides the decision framework for architecting AI solutions.

### 8. Multimodal AI: Beyond Text

**Read after:** Automation, AI Workflows, and AI Agents
